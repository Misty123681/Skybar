Admission Rules and Regulations

  • SKY2.0 reserves the right to refuse admission to any person for any reason, at any time.
  • Mixed groups and couples only will be permitted (this includes table reservations and guests lists)
  • Walk ins will be accepted based on door policy
  • Entrance strictly 21+ years (original identification documents only will be accepted)
  • Shorts, tracksuits or singlets are not permitted
  • Sports shoes, caps, open footwear (sandals, flip flops) are not permitted
  • Local attire and headscarves are not permitted
  • SKY2.0 applies a zero-tolerance policy for disruptive behavior. Such behavior will result in immediate expulsion from the venue.
  • Table reservations will be held until 11:30 PM.
  • Bar reservations will be held till 10:30 PM.
